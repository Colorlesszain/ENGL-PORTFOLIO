About Me

My name is Damion, and I am an undergrad freshman at UConn. I study computer science & engineering. I love D&D, Minecraft, and strategy games. I fell in love with coding early on because of gaming. Gaming was my stress outlet and still is, and because of the amazing games that I played growing up, I aspire to be able to create/code things that will inspire others as well. You will notice that this website is designed by me via HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I do many other websites in the same languages. I am also studying Python and have created my own memory game based on a handful of categories, such as anime, games, and movies. I also created platformer games, an infinite asteroid shooter, and many other simple /easy games just to start building my portfolio. What I lack in experience I earn back in enthusiasm and perseverance. I would be open to making more and new projects for more people.
